Surat Municipal Corporation Details

The Surat Municipal Corporation is a local self-government that administers the city of Surat. Established in 1966, SMC's primary responsibilities include maintaining public infrastructure, sanitation, health services, urban planning, and promoting development projects. SMC has been at the forefront of implementing smart city initiatives, providing citizens with high-quality services, and transforming Surat into a modern urban hub.

SMC's headquarters is located in Muglisara, and the organization operates through various departments, such as health, education, water supply, public transportation, and environment. With a focus on sustainability and cleanliness, the SMC has earned recognition as one of the cleanest cities in India.

Population of Surat City

As of the latest estimates, the population of Surat has crossed 69.4 lakhs (2021) , making it the second-largest city in Gujarat and the ninth-largest in India. The city has seen exponential growth due to its flourishing textile and diamond industries, which attract job seekers from across the country. The booming population has prompted the SMC to implement various urban development projects to accommodate this growth, ensuring a better quality of life for its citizens.

Surat Railway Station

Surat Railway Station is one of the busiest railway stations in Western India. Managed by the Western Railway Zone, it connects Surat with major cities across the country. The station handles thousands of passengers daily, reflecting the city's importance as a key commercial hub. In recent years, Surat Railway Station has undergone modernization, with improved facilities like digital ticketing, food courts, and enhanced safety measures, thanks to the efforts of the SMC and Indian Railways.

Surat Airport

Surat Airport has become a vital gateway for the city, facilitating both domestic and international travel. Located in Magdalla, the airport has seen rapid expansion to accommodate the growing passenger traffic. With connections to major Indian cities like Delhi, Mumbai, and Bangalore, Surat Airport has become a key driver for the city's economy, attracting more business and boosting tourism. The SMC is working closely with the Airport Authority of India to further expand the airport's infrastructure and flight routes.

Surat City Bus

The Surat City Bus service, managed by the Surat Municipal Corporation, provides a reliable and efficient public transportation system. The city bus network covers major parts of Surat, including residential, commercial, and industrial areas, ensuring that people can commute easily and affordably. The introduction of the Bus Rapid Transit System (BRTS) has further enhanced public transport, with dedicated lanes that reduce travel time. SMC's focus on improving public transportation has made commuting in Surat convenient, reducing the city's traffic congestion.

Future Plans and Development

Surat Municipal Corporation continues to work towards making the city more sustainable and livable. With projects focused on infrastructure development, smart city initiatives, waste management, and digital governance, Surat is on its way to becoming a model city in India. The SMC's continuous efforts to maintain cleanliness, improve public amenities, and embrace technological advancements ensure that Surat remains a leading urban center.
