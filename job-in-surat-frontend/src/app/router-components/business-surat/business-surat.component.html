Textile Industry

Known as the "Silk City," Surat is a top player in India's textile market. The city is famous for producing synthetic fabrics, silk, sarees, and embroidery. Surat's textile industry caters to domestic and international markets.

Key Sectors:

Fabric manufacturers, dyeing, printing, weaving, and embroidery.

Why It Matters: Surat's textile industry creates numerous jobs, from factory workers to designers, making it a cornerstone of the local economy.

Real Estate and Construction

The rapid urbanization in Surat has spurred growth in the construction and real estate sectors. From residential to commercial projects, the city's real estate is booming, driven by infrastructure development.

Key Sectors:

Residential and commercial real estate, construction, and property development.

Top Companies:

Happy Home Group, Rajhans Group, Shaligram Group, Gujarat Construction.

Why It Matters: Real estate and construction provide jobs in project management, sales, engineering, and more.

Gems and Jewellery

Beyond diamonds, Surat is a significant player in the broader gems and jewelry market. The city is known for its gold, silver, and diamond-studded jewelry.
