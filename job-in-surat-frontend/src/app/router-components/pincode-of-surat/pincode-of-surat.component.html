What is a Pin Code?

A Pin Code (Postal Index Number) is a six-digit code used by the postal department to identify specific areas and ensure the efficient delivery of mail. In Surat, like any other city in India, pin codes help in precisely locating areas, which is useful for everything from mail delivery to e-commerce logistics.
