Maps Of Surat Area

Surat is a vibrant city located on the southern bank of the Tapi River in Gujarat. Historically, Surat has been a key trading hub due to its strategic seaports, attracting merchants and traders from ancient times. Today, it remains well-connected to the rest of India, making it a crucial link for both commerce and tourism. Known as the "City of Flyovers,"

Surat boasts an impressive network of flyovers that ease travel across the city. In addition to its connectivity, Surat is rich in history, with attractions such as finely carved mosques, Parsi fire temples (agiaries), European tombs, and colonial-era houses, making it a fascinating destination for visitors.
